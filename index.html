Did You Know?

The first written evidence of coffee discovery was in the 15th century by Ahmed al-Ghaffar in Yemen, it was used by Sufis to stay up, and worship Allah. The shocking thing is that their method of roasting and brewing the coffee beans is almost like ours today! It also been said that Ali ben Omar of the Shadhili (a Sufist) was the one who introduce the coffee to Arabia. In 1670, for the first time, coffee was smuggled from the Middle East to India by Baba Budan (also a Sufist).

What is Coffee?

We get two green coffee beans from a red fruit called coffea, then we spoon them together, roast them, and they start to get rich brown. Coffee beans have more than caffeine, it has a thousand chemical compounds, all of them combined gives the body this crazy nice satisfying energy, and according to the professional tasters, Ethiopia has the best coffee in the world.
